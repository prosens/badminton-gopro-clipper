How to Clip & Export Matches

1. Find the start of a match. Play or scrub, then click I or Mark Start.
2. Find the end of the match. Click O or Mark End.
3. Click ➕ Add Split Segment to record the game. You'll see a scorecard card appear.
4. Fill in the players and score on the scorecard. Repeat for all games!
5. Click ⚡ Export to losslessly split and merge your matches in seconds!
